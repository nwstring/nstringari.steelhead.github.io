Steelhead are considered to be one of the most prized catches in all freshwater fishing, well known for their explosive fighting ability. When Steelhead are landed, and many are not, the sheer beauty and strength of the fish is enough to take your breath away.

Techniques

There are many different techniques which can be used to catch Steelhead trout. The most common are centerpinning, fly fishing and traditional spinning. Notice the different reels and casting styles in the clips below. Each technique provides the angler with a different advantage.

Season

The best time to catch one of these fish is normally at the middle of their migratory run. After the fall run starts around September, late October, all the way through the end of November is the best time to hook a Steelhead. If you can bare the freezing temps, try fishing December through February. It's one hell of an experience!

October - Altmar, NY

Regulations

If you're 16 years or older and are fishing for freshwater fish species by angling, you need a fishing license. The limit is 1 Steelhead per day but you can always catch and release.

Location

Here is the general location for some of the best Steelhead fishing in Upstate NY. Ask a fisherman where the fish are and they'll never give you an exact location...at least the smart one's won't. But I can tell you that yearling Steelhead are currently stocked in 22 tributaries of Lake Ontario. They range from small brush lined creeks to large powerful rivers.
